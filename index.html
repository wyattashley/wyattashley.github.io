2022 FIRST Robotics

Drive Train

In the realm of robotics and mechatronics, the Swerve Chassis stands as a remarkable innovation that combines precision, agility, and versatility. The Swerve Chassis imploys four rotational and translational axis which adds complexity when coming to comfiguring the forward and reverse kinematic equations. My role was to develop the equations for the forward and reverse kinematic so given only 3 input (left/right,forward/reverse,turning) all four wheels act in conjunction.

Launcher

For this season we dicided to implement a two wheel launcher which would allow for use to add precise amounts of spin on the game ball. I prototyped, assisted in design, and completed the programming for math involving launching the ball. Using variable spin and launch distance was able to make an adapive launcher that could score the ball from any place on the field.
